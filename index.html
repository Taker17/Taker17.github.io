How to Buy

Create a wallet

Begin by creating a wallet (Metamask is the most used one => Metamask.io)




Fund your wallet with ETH

Send ETH to your wallet from a Centralised Exchange (like Binance) or via Metamask itself




Connect your wallet on a DEX (Decentralised Exchange)

Use Uniswap (app.uniswap.org/) or 1inch (app.1inch.io)



Swap ETH for Karat

Copy the contract (0x7b731a3ff18bf1c9d0edc0d7bd4cd46423ec900d) and paste it on one of the DEX, then put your slippage at 7.5% and swap
